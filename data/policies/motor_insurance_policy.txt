DRIVEGUARD MOTOR INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: DG-MOTOR-2025-042
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Expiration Date: December 31, 2025

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SECTION 1 – DEFINITIONS

"Insured Vehicle" means the motor vehicle described in the policy declarations, including any permanently attached equipment.

"Named Insured" means the person or entity named in the policy declarations as the policyholder.

"Permissive User" means any person using the Insured Vehicle with the Named Insured's express or implied permission.

"Actual Cash Value" (ACV) means the replacement cost of the vehicle minus depreciation at the time of loss.

"Collision" means the upset of the Insured Vehicle or its impact with another vehicle or object.

"Comprehensive" means coverage for loss to the Insured Vehicle caused by events other than collision, including theft, vandalism, fire, natural disasters, falling objects, and animal strikes.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SECTION 2 – LIABILITY COVERAGE

2.1 Bodily Injury Liability
Coverage Limits: $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
Covers legal liability for bodily injury or death to others caused by the Named Insured or Permissive User while operating the Insured Vehicle. Includes defense costs and court-ordered settlements.

2.2 Property Damage Liability
Coverage Limit: $100,000 per accident
Covers legal liability for damage to another person's property caused by the operation of the Insured Vehicle.

2.3 Medical Payments Coverage
Coverage Limit: $10,000 per person
Covers reasonable medical expenses for the Named Insured and passengers injured in the Insured Vehicle, regardless of fault. Expenses must be incurred within 3 years of the accident.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SECTION 3 – PHYSICAL DAMAGE COVERAGE

3.1 Collision Coverage
Deductible: $500
Covers damage to the Insured Vehicle resulting from collision with another vehicle or object, including rollovers. Pays the lesser of repair cost or ACV minus the deductible.

3.2 Comprehensive Coverage
Deductible: $250
Covers loss to the Insured Vehicle from:
- Theft or attempted theft
- Vandalism or malicious mischief
- Fire, explosion, or lightning
- Windstorm, hail, flood, or earthquake
- Falling objects, including tree limbs
- Animal strikes (including deer)
- Riot or civil commotion
- Glass breakage (windshield repair with $0 deductible)

3.3 Rental Reimbursement
Coverage Limit: $50 per day, up to $1,500 per occurrence
Covers the cost of a rental vehicle while the Insured Vehicle is being repaired due to a covered loss.

3.4 Towing and Labor
Coverage Limit: $100 per occurrence
Covers towing charges and on-site labor costs (e.g., flat tire change, battery jump) when the vehicle is disabled.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SECTION 4 – UNINSURED / UNDERINSURED MOTORIST COVERAGE

4.1 Uninsured Motorist Bodily Injury
Coverage Limits: $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
Covers bodily injury to the Named Insured and passengers caused by an uninsured or hit-and-run driver.

4.2 Underinsured Motorist Coverage
Coverage Limits: $100,000 per person / $300,000 per accident
Provides additional coverage when the at-fault driver's liability limits are insufficient to cover the Named Insured's damages.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SECTION 5 – EXCLUSIONS

The following are NOT covered under this policy:

5.1 Intentional damage caused by the Named Insured or any Covered Person.

5.2 Damage occurring while the vehicle is used for hire (taxi, rideshare, delivery) unless a commercial endorsement is added.

5.3 Racing, speed contests, or use on a closed course or track.

5.4 Damage caused by war, military action, nuclear hazard, or radioactive contamination.

5.5 Wear and tear, mechanical breakdown, or electrical failure (covered under manufacturer warranty or mechanical breakdown insurance).

5.6 Custom equipment or modifications exceeding $1,000 unless specifically declared and endorsed.

5.7 Damage to tires unless caused by a covered collision or comprehensive event.

5.8 Loss of personal belongings inside the vehicle (may be covered under homeowner's/renter's insurance).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SECTION 6 – CLAIMS PROCEDURE

6.1 Reporting a Claim
All accidents must be reported within 24 hours by calling 1-800-555-DRIVE or filing online at www.driveguard.com/claims. A police report is required for theft, vandalism, or hit-and-run incidents.

6.2 Documentation Required
- Completed accident report form
- Police report number (if applicable)
- Photos of vehicle damage (at least 4 angles)
- Repair estimates from licensed body shops (minimum 2 estimates)
- Medical records and bills (for injury claims)

6.3 Inspection and Appraisal
DriveGuard may inspect the vehicle or assign an independent appraiser. For total loss claims, the ACV is determined using industry valuation guides and comparable vehicle data.

6.4 Settlement Process
Partial loss: Payment is made to the repair facility or the Named Insured upon completion of repairs. Total loss: Payment of ACV minus deductible and any outstanding liens. Settlement is typically issued within 15 business days of agreement.

SECTION 7 – PREMIUMS AND DISCOUNTS

Annual Premium: $1,800 (varies by driver profile and vehicle)

Available Discounts:
- Safe Driver (no accidents/violations in 3 years): 15%
- Multi-Vehicle: 10%
- Anti-Theft Device: 5%
- Defensive Driving Course: 10%
- Good Student (GPA 3.0+, under 25): 8%
- Bundling with Home Insurance: 12%
- Pay-in-Full: 5%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SECTION 8 – CANCELLATION AND RENEWAL

Either party may cancel with 30 days written notice. Refund for mid-term cancellation is calculated on a pro-rata basis. Policy is guaranteed renewable provided premiums are paid. Rates may be adjusted at renewal based on claims history, driving record, and vehicle age.
